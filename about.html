About Me

This is what I do.

These experiences have equipped me with a solid foundation in software development methodologies and best practices.

In addition to my dedication to software development, I have a strong passion for reading, sports, and traveling. Engaging in sports not only helps me stay physically fit but also nurtures teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. It provides me with inspiration and fresh perspectives that I bring back to my work.

With a strong foundation in CI/CD development, I leverage my expertise in writing Python scripts, Bash scripts, Kubernetes, Docker, Spinnaker, and more to drive innovation and efficiency in software delivery processes. I am driven to contribute to innovative projects and collaborate with like-minded professionals. I look forward to connecting with individuals who share similar passions and exchanging experiences that foster personal and professional growth.
